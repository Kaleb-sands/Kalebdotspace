WARNING: This trail has not been skied before, only hiked. Proceed with extreme caution.

Description:

This trail can be found off of White Cap. Start by hiking up from the chair to the top of the peak. Turn left and look for the hiking trail. There should be markings on the trees going down. Follow this down dropping you at the top of the Little White Cap Quad.
