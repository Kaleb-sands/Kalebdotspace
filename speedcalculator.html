Speed Calculator

Welcome to the Speed Calculator. This calculates the top speed of a vehicle based off of the parameters below. Calculation:

RPM of wheel = RPM / ratio of Sprocket to Clutch

Inches per minute = RPM of wheel * circumfrence of wheel (diameter * pi)

Speed in miles per hour = (Inches per minute * 60 minutes) / Inches in a mile (63360)
